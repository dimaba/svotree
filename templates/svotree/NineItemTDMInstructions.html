In this task you have been randomly paired with another person, whom we will refer to simply as the other. This other person is someone you do not know and will remain mutually anonymous. All of your choices are completely confidential. You will be making a series of choices about allocating resources between you and this other person. You will indicate your choices by selecting either column A, B, or C on each of the following directions. Your decisions will yield money for both yourself and the other person.

In the example below, a person has chosen to distribute money so that he/she receives 100 points, whereas the anonymous other person receives 50 points. The person has marked his/her choice by circling the corresponding column B. As you can see, your choices will influence both the amount of money you receive and the amount of money the other receives. Before you begin, please keep in mind that there are no right or wrong answers - for each question choose the option that you prefer most. When you have made all your decisions, press the Continue button at the bottom of the screen to complete this decision task.
