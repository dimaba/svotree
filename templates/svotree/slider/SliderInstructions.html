In this task, imagine that you have been randomly paired with another person, whom we will refer to as the other. This other person is someone you do not know and will remain mutually anonymous. All of your choices are completely confidential.
You will be making a series of decisions about allocating resources between you and this other person. For each of the following questions, please indicate the distribution you prefer most by moving the slider along the scale to your preferred payoff allocation.
You can only make one selection for each question. Your decisions will yield money for both yourself and the other person.

There are no right or wrong answers, this is all about personal preferences. After you have made your decision, make it final by pressing the Confirm button to the right of the scale. As you can see, your choices will influence both the amount of points you receive and the amount of points the other receives.
